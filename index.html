Starting Your Fitness Journey

The 2 main components you will need to focus on are

  1. Lifting weights
  2. Nutrition

Lifting

The easiest split to start out with is Push, Pull, Legs.

That would look something like this:

Day 1: Push day (working the chest, triceps, and shoulders)

Day 2: Pull day (working the back and biceps)

Day 3: Legs (working the quads, hamstrings, and glutes)

Day 4: Rest

Nutrition

What you eat and how much of it will arguably get you more results than the gym will.
